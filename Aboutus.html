History

Since 1991

  1. Establishment : Royal Enfield was originally founded in 1901 as "Enfield Manufacturing Co. Ltd." in Redditch, England. They initially manufactured bicycles and firearm components.

  2. Motorcycles Begin : In 1909, Royal Enfield produced their first motorcycle powered by a 2.25 hp engine.

  3. Growth and Innovation : Over the years, Royal Enfield continued to innovate and expand their motorcycle lineup.

  4. Modern Era : In recent years, Royal Enfield has experienced a resurgence in popularity, both in India and internationally. They have modernized their motorcycles while maintaining their classic design.

  5. Global Presence : The company has a global presence and is recognized for its retro-style motorcycles and a strong enthusiast community.
